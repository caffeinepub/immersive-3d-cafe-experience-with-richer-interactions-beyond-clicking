- No attribution is required, though it is appreciated.

## Model Sources

- **Poly Pizza**: https://poly.pizza - A community-driven repository of free 3D models, many from the original Google Poly project
- Models are stored locally in `frontend/public/assets/models/` and are not fetched from external sources at runtime

## Notes

The actual GLB files should be downloaded from the sources above (or similar CC0 repositories) and placed in:
- `frontend/public/assets/models/humans/` (human-01.glb, human-02.glb, human-03.glb)
- `frontend/public/assets/models/dogs/` (dog-01.glb, dog-02.glb)
- `frontend/public/assets/models/furniture/` (table-chair-set.glb)

If the specific models listed are unavailable, any CC0-licensed low-poly models of similar subjects can be substituted.
